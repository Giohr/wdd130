Overview

Purpose

Our website dedicates to make lists of quotes than can be added to your google calendar or any type of calendar. These quotes can vary in length, topic, language and so much more. This calendar is made for us by us.

Audience

Our audience is mostly adults who work with calendars or use them regularly.

Content

Home page (inspiration and examples)

The home page will display three sections in which will make an overview of what the site is about. The first section is the logo itself, showing what the site is specific about. The second section will be a collection of individual quotes, and the third will be comments and what people did in the site to help build the quote calendars.
