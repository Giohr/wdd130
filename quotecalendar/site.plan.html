Overview

Purpose

Our website dedicates to make lists of quotes than can be added to your google calendar or any type of calendar. These quotes can vary in length, topic, language and so much more. This calendar is made for us by us.

Audience

Our audience is mostly adults who work with calendars or use them regularly.

Content

Home page

The home page will display three sections in which will make an overview of what the site is about. The first section is a big nice logo that shows what the site is specific about. It is called 'Quote Calendar' for people to understand it quickly. The second section will be a collection of some individual quotes that can vary from person, year, theme, languague and length. The third oage will be comments and what people did in the site (specifically the calendars) to help build trust with the quote calendars.

Images for the Home page (inspiration and examples)

Quotes

The Quotes page will be specifically about individual quotes display through all the page. There will be no order in any of them. You can scroll left or right and they will move.

Calendars

The Calendars page will display the calendars per year and per month with a link to copy to your own google calendar. This is a free download. People can use it freely and change it for others to see and find interesting quotes.
