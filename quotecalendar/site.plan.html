Purpose

Audience

Our audience is mostly adults who work with calendars or use them regularly.

Content

Home page

The home page will display three main sections (navbar and footer will be part of all the pages). The first section is the hero image where the name (Quote Calendar) of the webpage appears. It won’t have any button or hover because it’s purpose is to show the name and introduce the user what it is about.
The second section is a quick show how and what the quotes are about. The grid will have a button that access to the Quotes Page where all the most important or interesting quotes are. There won’t be anything to do more than that.
The third and last section is a little display of the comments made in the Calendars Page. These comments will show and invite the user to the active community who adds quotes to the calendars and share with each other.
There is no ‘Contact Us’ section because there’s no need to as it is a collaborative work. But in the footer are links to some social media pages.
The navigation bar is the one on top of everything and it shows the logo and the quick access to the other two pages and the Home itself.

Images for the Home page

Quotes

The Quotes Page (the second page) differs from the Calendars Page on the quantity and types of quotes. Not all the quotes scheduled for each day in the calendars will appear in the Quotes Page as they are a mix of the webpage and the contribution of the community. Those quotes (the Quotes Page) are from the webpage itself and they are scheduled for different random days in the calendars.
The navigation bar shows the same information as the Home Page except this highlights that it is in the Quotes Page. The footer remains the same.
There is an initial main image with a title that says, “Welcome to the quotes” and a short thought that describes the beauty of quotes and their impact in our lives.
After these two sections, all the quotes will be displayed on the Quotes Page like a museum for the user to look and read the quotes from each person. The quotes arrangement is random as they vary from person, length, color, even language. There may be many quotes from the same author as from the same theme. The user won’t be able to copy the quotes as they are images, but at the end of the page, there will be a link to all the quotes listed and ready to be used.

Images for the Page 2: Quotes

Calendars

The Calendars Page is the third and last child page from the Home Page. As the user could see in the comments sections of the Home Page, the Calendars Page does show comments on the monthly calendars on one side of the page.
The navigation bar shows the same information with the exception that it highlights that the user is in fact in the Calendars Page. The footer also shows the same links to the social media pages and the author of the webpages.
The first section is like the second section as they show the same design of image on one side, and an invitation on the other side that links you to another part where the calendars are. The first section is an invitation to the calendars per year and the second is another invitation to see the calendars per month (this year’s months).
The third section is the monthly calendar with a link to download it for free. All the calendars’ links can be added to the user’s own personal calendar. When you click on the monthly calendar, it will go to the place where the calendars per month are and it will show the monthly calendar with all the comments on the sides and an invitation to edit the calendar by yourself. You cannot edit over other’s work but if there’s something inappropriate, you can report to the creator and it will be reviewed.
